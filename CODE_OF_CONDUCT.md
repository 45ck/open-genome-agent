# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Prioritizing evidence, safety, and privacy when discussing genomics workflows

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Harassment in public or private spaces
- Publishing someone else's private information without permission
- Pressuring contributors to share sensitive genome-derived data

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. Reports will be reviewed and handled promptly and fairly.
